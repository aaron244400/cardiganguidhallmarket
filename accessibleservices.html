Accessible Services

Cardigan Guidhall Market is committed to creating a welcoming and accessible environment for all visitors, ensuring comfort, inclusion, and ease of movement throughout the venue.

×

The venue includes wheelchair-friendly entrances, accessible pathways, and designated areas to support visitors with mobility needs during events and gatherings.

Accessible washroom facilities and visitor support services are also available to help create a safe, comfortable, and inclusive experience for everyone.
